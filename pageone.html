FutureTech was founded in 2010 by a team of passionate engineers. It began as a startup in a basement and quickly evolved into a global leader in IoT solutions.

  1. 2010 – Company founded
  2. 2012 – First product launched
  3. 2016 – Expanded internationally
  4. 2023 – Reached 10 million users worldwide
