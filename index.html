About NovaEdge Technologies

Innovating tomorrow’s solutions—today.

Founded in 2012, NovaEdge Technologies began as a small startup in Chicago with a big idea: connect the world smarter and faster.

In just a few years, we expanded into Europe, Asia, and beyond—fueling growth in smart infrastructure, logistics, and data analytics.

Key Milestones

  1. 2012 – Company founded
  2. 2014 – First product release: NovaLink
  3. 2017 – Global expansion to 10 countries
  4. 2023 – Surpassed 10M connected users
