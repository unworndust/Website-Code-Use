About Coltivare

Innovating tomorrow’s solutions—today.

Coltivare was founded in 1825. Our mission is to offer leading edge products to enable the future of farming.

Our History

Coltivare started as a small farm in Indiana. Our heritage has defined us since the beginning, and we provide leading edge products and services alongside our 200 year old knowledge base.

Key Milestones

  1. 1825 – Farm founded
  2. 1887 – First plow sold
  3. 1950 – First tractor sold
  4. 1995 – Reached 50+ generations of farmers

What We Value

  • Integrity
  • Customer-first thinking
  • Sustainability
  • Teamwork
