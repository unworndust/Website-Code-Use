About NovaEdge Technologies

Our Story

NovaEdge Technologies was founded in 2012 with a mission to build smarter, more sustainable systems for industries across the globe.

From humble beginnings in a shared workspace in Chicago, our journey has been one of bold ideas, tough challenges, and breakthrough technologies. Today, NovaEdge powers infrastructure, health systems, and logistics operations in more than 45 countries.

Key Milestones

  1. 2012 – NovaEdge is founded
  2. 2014 – Launch of first AI-driven logistics platform
  3. 2017 – Expansion into Europe and Asia
  4. 2021 – Reached $100M in annual revenue
